# Module 1: Physical AI Foundations

This module introduces the fundamental concepts of Physical AI and embodied intelligence, bridging the gap between digital intelligence and real-world robotic systems.

## Learning Objectives

After completing this module, you will be able to:

- Define Physical AI and distinguish it from traditional digital AI
- Explain the principles of embodied cognition and intelligence
- Understand how AI systems transition from digital intelligence to embodied agents
- Identify key challenges in creating physically grounded AI systems
- Recognize the relationship between perception, action, and learning in embodied systems

## Module Structure

This module contains the following chapters:

- **Chapter 1: Overview** - Introduction to Physical AI concepts and definitions
- **Chapter 2: Embodied AI** - Principles of embodied cognition and intelligence
- **Chapter 3: Digital-to-Physical Transition** - From simulation to real-world deployment
- **Chapter 4: Learning Objectives Review** - Assessment and key takeaways

## Prerequisites

Before starting this module, you should have:

- Basic understanding of artificial intelligence concepts
- Familiarity with robotics terminology (optional but helpful)
- Background in computer science fundamentals

## Getting Started

Begin with Chapter 1: Overview to establish the foundational concepts that will be built upon throughout this textbook.
